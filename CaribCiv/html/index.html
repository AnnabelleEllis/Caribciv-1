Caribbean Integration Network

“Achieving regional integration one step at a time”

Caribbean Integration Network, CIN, is an organization which seeks to influence two or more nation-states to agree to co-operate and work closely together to achieve peace, stability and wealth. Through promoting economic and political integration, the co-operating countries of CIN achieve such a unity where all barriers to the movements of labour, goods and capital are removed. Thus, forming a newer and friendlier Caribbean Family.
